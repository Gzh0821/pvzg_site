**My simple GE balance mod that rebalances a plethora plants in a way that each plant is a worthwhile and fun pick in the seed selection.**

_Some stand-out features in the mod:_

- Thought-out and carefully rebalanced/reworked plants to ensure they all of their own strengths and weaknesses, with many rebalances aiming to build off of VNL/be as close to their original form as possible, yet somewhat different and funner to use
- 2 or more unlockable adventure plants in every world to make the expansions extra entertaining
- More starting plants for every endless zone
- Reworked and rebalanced levels (like WW27, WW35, FF31, etc.)
- A comprehensive in-game almanac including more stats like fire intervals, splash damage, sun production rates, etc.
