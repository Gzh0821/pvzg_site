## Sandy's Pvz2: Rebloomed

Sandy's Pvz2: Rebloomed is a PvZ2 Gardendless campaign mod by Sandy. Its `pack.json` describes it as a large revamp of PvZ2 Gardendless.

## Features

- Reworks the campaign flow, with the current version reaching Pirate Seas Day 5

- The README marks vanilla difficulty as C and lists harder routes for players who have completed the original PvZ2 before

- Includes world-order changes, starting from Players House and Ancient Egypt before moving into later worlds

## Note

`pack.json` targets game version `0.7.1` and requires GP-Next `>=1.0.0`.

## Download

You can download the mod from [GitHub](https://github.com/SandyBrawlStars/Pvz2Rebloom).
